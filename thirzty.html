Welcome to Thirzty

Thirzty is a Chennai based tech startup that connects household drinking water can providers with consumers based on their pin codes.

We provide a convenient and hassle-free solution for accessing drinking water. You can notify the provider of your specific water needs and make online payments without the need for cash.

We also offer subscription-based delivery options on a weekly, monthly, or yearly basis. You can track your consumption stats and upcoming payments on our dashboard.

Our Services

  • Home delivery of drinking water
  • Pin code based provider search
  • Online payment solution
  • Subscription-based delivery options
  • Dashboard for tracking consumption and payments

How It Works

  1. Sign up and enter your pin code to find providers in your area.
  2. Select the size and urgency of your water needs and place your order.
  3. Make online payments securely without the need for cash.
  4. Track your consumption stats and upcoming payments on our dashboard.

About Us

Thirzty is a Chennai-based tech startup that connects households with drinking water can providers. Our platform allows consumers to find water can providers in their area based on pin codes, and enables them to place orders for water cans of a specific size and urgency. We also offer subscription-based water can delivery services that can be scheduled weekly, monthly, or yearly. Our online payment solutions eliminate the need for cash payments, and our dashboard provides consumers with consumption statistics and payment information.
